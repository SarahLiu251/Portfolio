Hydroponics Tower Project

This project was done under the Cipass Apprenticeship in a team of 6. The goal was to develop an self sustaining growing system that would encourage more locally produce. The use of 3D printing helped in rapid prototyping to optimize the design. Various sensors were used to help the self-sustaining aspect. Deisgn choices such as an motorized platform and mobility were made to fit that of many urban homes.
