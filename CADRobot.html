Reverse Engineering CAD Project

This project was done my first semester at City College of New York for an Computer-aided drafting course. In a team of 4, we utilized Solidworks as a main mode of drafting the parts of an toy RC Robot in order to determine ways to improve its efficiency, function and cost. In additions to CAD we drafted hand drawn views of the robot and from the precise measurements taken. FDM printing was used as a way to showcase potential redesign.
